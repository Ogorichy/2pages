About Us

All Lines Exposure & Integrated Services Limited, ALEXIS is an out-of-home advertising practitioner for about two decades. As part of its growth, ALEXIS have been able to expand its static billboards in 5 different commercial cities in Nigeria i.e. Lagos, Ibadan, Port Harcourt, Onitsha and Kano etc. ALEXIS static billboards Pan Nigeria are located on a prime route and are highly visible for the target audience with elevated exposure for brand messages. Over the years, ALEXIS have been able to work with big brands building a strong relationship with our clients, media buying agencies, OOH media companies providing uncluttered views of campaigns on our boards, unique reach of brand messages and pocket friendly rates.
